About This Website

Welcome to my website. I have created this website because of my love of astronomy and astrophotography and I want to share many of the images that I have taken through my telescopes. I created this site because I love the hobby of star-gazing and astrophotography and I like to share it with others. The source code for this website is free to use and is open-source under the Apache 2.0 License. The link to the GitHub repo is in the footers. Feel free to clone the code for this website and use it as part of your own projects. A log of the patch notes can be found at this link, Patch Notes.

Licensing

All images posted here are covered under the creative commons attribution 4.0 international license. This means that you can share the images that are on this site and you can remix or build upon the images here even to use in commercial products as long as you give credit to me as the source of the images.
